Our Story

Why We Started

Seb & Bayor was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between quality pharmaceutical care and the everyday Nigerian patient. In a healthcare landscape where access to verified medications and professional guidance remains a challenge, we saw an opportunity to do things differently.

We started as a retail pharmacy committed to patient-centered dispensing and counseling. As we grew, we recognized that digital innovation could extend our reach beyond our physical walls — leading to the creation of AnythingDrugs, our e-pharmacy platform that brings pharmacist-verified medications to patients anywhere in Nigeria.

Today, we operate at the intersection of traditional pharmacy practice and modern healthcare technology, serving patients through retail, digital, and professional pharmacy services.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, accessible, and affordable pharmaceutical care to patients across Nigeria through licensed retail pharmacy practice, innovative digital health solutions, and professional clinical services — ensuring every patient receives the care and guidance they deserve.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted integrated healthcare company — combining physical pharmacy, e-pharmacy, drug information, and digital health services into a unified platform that sets the standard for pharmaceutical care in Africa.
