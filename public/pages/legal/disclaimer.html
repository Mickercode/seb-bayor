Self-Medication Warning

Seb & Bayor Pharmaceutical Ltd does not recommend self-medication. Prescription medications are dispensed only upon pharmacist verification of a valid prescription. Over-the-counter medications should be used as directed and for their intended purpose. If symptoms persist, consult a healthcare professional.

Adverse Reactions

If you experience any adverse reaction to a medication purchased from us, discontinue use immediately and seek medical attention. You should also report adverse drug reactions to NAFDAC through their reporting system, and notify our pharmacist so we can assist you.

Emergency Situations

In case of a medical emergency, do not use this platform. Call your local emergency services or proceed to the nearest hospital emergency department immediately.
